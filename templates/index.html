Breast cancer is a silent killer, affecting 1 in 8 women around the world.
Here at ABCD, we are committed to improving the detection and treatment of breast cancer, providing everyone with a chance to tackle cancer before it tackles them. Using state of the art Machine Learning, we have developed a software for both doctors and patients to detect breast cancer based on easily-recorded symptoms and characteristics. With our web-based software, millions can contribute data at a time, improving detection confidence exponentially!
